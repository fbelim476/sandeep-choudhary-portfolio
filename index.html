Key Projects & Impact

Healthcare Process Optimization (DMAIC)

Problem:

Inefficient patient admission process leading to long wait times and reduced satisfaction.

Approach:

Implemented DMAIC methodology to analyze and redesign the process, focusing on key bottlenecks and waste reduction.

Outcome:

Reduced patient admission time by 30%, increasing patient satisfaction by 15% and improving staff productivity.

IT Service Workflow Optimization (Agile + Six Sigma)

Problem:

Slow IT service request fulfillment and high error rates impacting operational efficiency.

Approach:

Integrated Agile and Six Sigma principles to streamline workflow, automate repetitive tasks, and implement continuous feedback loops.

Outcome:

Decreased service request resolution time by 25% and reduced critical errors by 20%, enhancing IT service delivery.

Cost Center Optimization & Workforce Efficiency

Problem:

Rising operational costs and underutilized workforce capacity in a key cost center.

Approach:

Conducted a comprehensive analysis of cost drivers and workforce allocation, implementing targeted efficiency programs and training initiatives.

Outcome:

Achieved 18% cost reduction and improved workforce utilization by 22%, delivering significant financial savings.

Contact Center Process Modernization

Problem:

Outdated contact center processes leading to high agent attrition and low customer satisfaction scores.

Approach:

Modernized call routing, integrated new CRM tools, and introduced advanced agent training with a focus on first-call resolution.

Outcome:

Increased customer satisfaction by 20% and reduced agent turnover by 15%, enhancing service quality and retention.

Enterprise PMO Governance Setup

Problem:

Lack of standardized project management processes across the enterprise, leading to inconsistent project outcomes.

Approach:

Established an Enterprise Project Management Office (EPMO) with clear governance frameworks, standardized methodologies, and centralized reporting.

Outcome:

Improved project success rates by 25% and enhanced portfolio visibility, leading to better strategic alignment and resource utilization.

AI-Enabled Quality Monitoring Framework

Problem:

Manual and time-consuming quality monitoring processes in a large operational unit.

Approach:

Designed and implemented an AI-powered framework for automated quality monitoring, leveraging machine learning for anomaly detection and performance insights.

Outcome:

Automated 70% of quality checks, reducing monitoring effort by 50% and increasing detection accuracy by 30%.

CX Analytics & VOC Transformation

Problem:

Limited understanding of customer pain points and lack of actionable insights from customer feedback.

Automation-Led Cost Reduction Program
